## üìê Step-by-Step: Create Icon in Canva

### Step 1: Set Up Canvas

1. Go to [canva.com](https://www.canva.com)
2. Click **"Create a design"**
3. Choose **"Custom size"**
4. Enter: **1024 x 1024 pixels**
5. Click **"Create new design"**

### Step 2: Design Your Icon

**Option A: Use Template**
1. Search for "app icon" in templates
2. Browse children/family themed templates
3. Click on one you like
4. Customize colors, text, images

**Option B: Start from Scratch**
1. Add background color (your brand color)
2. Add shapes or images
3. Add text if needed (keep it simple!)
4. Add your logo if you have one

### Step 3: Design Tips

- **Keep it Simple**: Works at small sizes
- **Use Your Brand Colors**: Match your app's color scheme
- **High Contrast**: Light on dark or dark on light
- **Center Everything**: Important content in center 80%
- **No Small Text**: Won't be readable at small sizes

### Step 4: Export

1. Click **"Download"** (top right)
2. Select **"PNG"** format
3. Make sure size is **1024x1024**
4. Click **"Download"**

## üé® Design Specifications

### Technical Requirements

- **Size**: Exactly 1024x1024 pixels
- **Format**: PNG (no transparency)
- **Color Space**: RGB
- **File Size**: Under 500KB recommended

### Design Guidelines

- **Shape**: Square (iOS adds rounded corners)
- **Safe Zone**: Keep important content in center 80%
- **Colors**: High contrast, vibrant
- **Style**: Simple, clean, modern
- **Readability**: Should be recognizable at 60x60 pixels
